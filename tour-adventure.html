Tour Details

Kolkata – Gateway to the East
  • Dive into the chaos of vibrant street markets and colonial alleys
  • Explore the bustling Mullick Ghat Flower Market at dawn
  • Savor your first authentic taste of spicy Bengali cuisine
  • Optional: Discover the colorful artisan quarter of Kumartuli
Darjeeling – Queen of the Hills
  • Ride the legendary Darjeeling Himalayan “Toy Train” through stunning mountain landscapes
  • Trek through lush hills to a working tea plantation and taste fresh mountain tea
  • Catch the breathtaking sunrise over the Himalayas from Tiger Hill
  • Explore the iconic Peace Pagoda and soak in panoramic views
Sandakphu Trek – Himalayan High
  • Challenge yourself with a 4-day trek through rhododendron forests and remote mountain villages
  • Stand atop Sandakphu (3,636m) and take in jaw-dropping views of Kanchenjunga and Everest
  • Sleep in rustic tea houses and bond with fellow trekkers
  • Experience epic mountain sunrises and star-filled skies far from city lights
North Sikkim – Remote Himalayan Wilderness
  • Embark on an exciting road trip through North Sikkim with a knowledgeable local guide
  • Navigate winding mountain roads to Lachung and the breathtaking Yumthang Valley
  • Soak your muscles in natural hot springs with panoramic mountain views
  • Encounter traditional Buddhist villages and dramatic landscapes
  • Embrace the wild, untouched beauty of Sikkim’s alpine terrain
Manas National Park – Assam’s Jungle Frontier
  • Stay in a relaxed jungle retreat in a small village, immersing yourself in authentic village life
  • Embark on an early sunrise jeep safari to spot tigers, elephants, and rhinos in their natural habitat
  • Enjoy guided nature walks and the peaceful atmosphere of the forest
  • Take a well-deserved break here to recharge your energy for the rest of the adventure
Cherrapunjee – Land of Giants
  • Trek to awe-inspiring living root bridges, ancient natural wonders crafted by locals
  • Chase thunderous waterfalls like Nohkalikai and Seven Sisters Falls
  • Explore one of the world’s wettest places, with rugged terrain and dramatic cliffs
  • Optional: Camp out under the stars or stay in a rustic eco-lodge
Guwahati – Adventure’s End
  • Visit the iconic Kamakhya Temple overlooking the city
  • Take a scenic boat ride on the mighty Brahmaputra River at sunset
  • Celebrate your epic journey with a hearty Assamese feast by the river
