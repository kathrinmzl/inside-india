Tour Details

Delhi – Dive Into the Heartbeat of India
  • Experience the bustling chaos of Delhi with a street walking tour through Chandi Chowk, tasting the best street food and exploring the world’s largest spice market
  • Visit iconic landmarks like India Gate and the colonial-era boulevards
  • Enjoy a relaxed start to your journey by soaking in the city’s energy before heading deeper into India’s culture
Agra – Home of the Taj Mahal
  • Witness the breathtaking sunrise over the iconic Taj Mahal, marveling at its timeless beauty and romantic legends
  • Explore the Agra Fort, a Mughal masterpiece
  • Visit Mehtab Bagh for a scenic view of the Taj
  • Optional marble inlay workshop experience
Jaipur – The Pink City
  • Kick off your adventure with an exhilarating auto rickshaw tour through Jaipur’s vibrant streets, discovering colorful markets and iconic landmarks
  • Discover the majestic Amber Fort
  • Walk through the City Palace and Jantar Mantar observatory
  • Snap photos at the iconic Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds)
  • Visit a traditional block-printing workshop
Pushkar – Sacred Town on the Edge of the Desert
  • Visit the Brahma Temple, one of the few in the world
  • Browse the vibrant Pushkar Bazaar
  • Experience the legendary Pushkar Camel Fair – a colorful desert festival of livestock trading, folk performances, and cultural celebration
  • Attend an Aarti at the holy lake – a spiritual ritual at sunset
Jodhpur – The Blue City
  • Tour the imposing Mehrangarh Fort
  • Explore the winding streets of the blue-painted old town
  • Join a local family who runs a spice shop for an immersive cooking class, discovering authentic Rajasthani flavors and secret recipes
  • Sip chai with locals at a rooftop café overlooking the fort
Jaisalmer – The Golden City in the Thar Desert
  • Visit a desert village to learn about local life and enjoy a traditional folk dance performance
  • Explore the golden Jaisalmer Fort, a living fort still inhabited
  • Admire intricate carvings at Patwon Ki Haveli
  • Take a camel safari into the Thar Desert with a night under the stars
Udaipur – The City of Lakes
  • Boat ride on Lake Pichola past the Lake Palace
  • Visit the regal City Palace complex
  • Take a sunrise scooter tour to Badi Lake – a serene escape with panoramic hill views
  • Enjoy a traditional Rajasthani thali dinner with sunset views
