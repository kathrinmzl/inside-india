Tour Details

Delhi – Dive Into the Heartbeat of India
  • Arrival & soft cultural landing
  • Guided street walk through Chandni Chowk
  • Taste India’s best street food
  • Visit Khari Baoli – the largest spice market in the world
  • First immersion into India’s vibrant chaos
Haridwar – Gateway to the Gods
  • Sacred Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri
  • Sunrise river bathing ritual
  • Cable car to Mansa Devi & Chandi Devi Temples
  • Optional Vedic astrology reading with local priest
Rishikesh – The Yoga Capital of the World
  • Sunrise yoga & pranayama by the Ganges
  • Attend satsangs and kirtans in spiritual ashrams
  • Take part in ashram life at Parmarth Niketan
  • Explore the mystical Beatles Ashram (Chaurasi Kutia)
  • Witness the Ganga Aarti at Triveni Ghat
Dharamshala – In the Presence of the Dalai Lama
  • Meditation at the Tsuglagkhang Temple Complex
  • Tibetan monk chanting session
  • Visit the Norbulingka Institute for sacred Tibetan art
  • Silent walks in the forests of McLeod Ganj
Amritsar – The Light of Devotion
  • Golden Temple visit at sunrise or sunset
  • Participate in Langar – the world's largest community meal
  • Witness the Palki Sahib ceremony
  • Reflect by moonlight at the Amrit Sarovar (holy pool)
